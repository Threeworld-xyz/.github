## I.&nbsp;&nbsp;The premise

Owning real estate used to mean a six-figure down payment, a mortgage broker, and a row of signatures. Fractional ownership changed that. You buy a slice of a property — like buying shares in a company — and earn your share of the rent.

The problem: those slices live across a growing pile of platforms, each with its own login, dashboard, and quirks.

**Threeworld** is the unified atlas that brings them together.

## V.&nbsp;&nbsp;Trust, by design

Threeworld is **read-only by architecture**.

We don't custody money. We don't execute trades. We don't sign anything on your behalf. Your funds and your transactions stay with the regulated platforms that issue them — we surface what's available across the field, and unify what you already own.

If we get hacked tomorrow, the worst case is someone sees your portfolio. They cannot move it.
