# Threeworld

**Like Zillow, but for owning property shares. No $1M down payment.**

Threeworld is a unified aggregator for fractional real estate investing. We pull opportunities from every major platform into one place, so you can discover, compare, track, and manage your property investments without juggling a dozen logins.

[**threeworld.xyz →**](https://threeworld.xyz)

## What we're building

Real estate investing used to need a lot. Now it doesn't. Fractional ownership lets you buy a slice of a property — like buying shares in a company — and earn your share of the rental income.

The problem: those opportunities are scattered across a growing list of platforms, each with its own dashboard, its own portfolio, its own quirks. We're fixing that.

Threeworld is the single command center for the fractional real estate world.

## What you can do on Threeworld

- **Marketplace** — Browse hundreds of fractional properties across every platform. Filter by price, yield, type, and location.
- **3D Globe Explorer** — Discover properties geographically on an interactive globe. Click a marker, preview the listing.
- **Portfolio Tracker** — One unified view of everything you own, across every platform you've connected. Dividends, returns, exports for tax season.
- **Analytics Dashboard** — Market-wide stats, yield distributions, top performers — across all platforms, not just one.
- **Watchlist** — Save properties and get notified when prices move.
- **AI Assistant** — Ask anything about a property, the market, or how fractional investing works.

## Platforms we aggregate

We bring together listings and portfolio data from the platforms shaping the space:

- **Arrived Homes** — SEC-regulated single-family rentals (Reg A+)
- **GetStake** — Fractional properties in emerging markets
- **Lofty** — Tokenized real estate on the blockchain
- **RealT** — Blockchain-based fractional ownership

…with more on the way.

## How it works

1. **Sign up free** — Email and 18+. No credit card, no KYC upfront.
2. **Browse the world** — Globe, marketplace, analytics — explore everything before you commit a dollar.
3. **Connect your accounts** — Read-only OAuth into the platforms you already use. We can never move funds or place trades on your behalf. We only ever look.
4. **Watch your portfolio grow** — Every property, every dividend, every platform — in one place.

## Trust, by design

Threeworld is **read-only by architecture**. We don't custody your money. We don't execute trades. We surface what's available and unify what you already own. Your funds and transactions stay with the regulated platforms that issue them.

## Who it's for

First-time investors looking for a way in that doesn't require six figures. Seasoned investors who want one dashboard instead of ten. Anyone who's curious about owning real estate but never thought it was for them.

Real estate, finally for everyone.
